Day care

Schedule and tuition

01

Morning Session

Part-time Montessori programme nurturing independence, concentration, and foundational life skills. Children engage in practical life exercises, sensorial activities, language, and mathematics exercises, guided by experienced Montessori teachers.

7:30 am – 12:00 noon

Tsh 1,500,000 per instalment (Total 4,500,000 yearly)

02

Extended Day

Longer hours with enriched activities, guided learning, and supervised play. Children explore creative arts, music, movement, and mindfulness alongside Montessori exercises, promoting emotional, social, and intellectual development.

7:30 am – 2:00 pm

Tsh 2,000,000 per instalment (Total 6,000,000 yearly)

03

Full Day

Comprehensive full-day care including extended learning, outdoor play, and enrichment activities. Children develop independence, social skills, motor abilities, and a love for learning in a safe, supportive environment.

7:30 am – 5:00 pm

Tsh 3,000,000 per instalment (Total 9,000,000 yearly)

We welcome kids into our 2-4’s program based on their birth year.
