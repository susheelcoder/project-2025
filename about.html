About us

DMRC is operated to provide children with the highest quality early learning environment possible. Our research-based Montessori programme follows the guiding principles of the Association Montessori Internationale (AMI). Our mission is to nurture the curiosity and wonder that inspires learning so that children develop a deep understanding of the foundational skills essential to fulfilling their human potential.

We all need support through the most important period of our children: the early years
