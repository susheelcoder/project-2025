8-Day Kilimanjaro Climb: Lemosho Route

Tour Overview

The Kilimanjaro Lemosho route is considered to be the most scenic of all routes. It is less traveled but arguably more picturesque, and at the same time somewhat lengthier and more costly. The Lemosho Gate, which requires further driving from Moshi town, sits on the western mountainside. It was created as an alternative to the Shira route. The initial three days of this route match with the eight-day Lemosho route and is customizable for people looking to ascend through the Western Breach and spend a night at Crater Camp. This area has an increased level of risk. Guests are required to sign a disclaimer before starting the trek. Day 0 - ARRIVALS When you get to Kilimanjaro Airport (JRO), I will meet you right after that and we can drive for an hour to the hotel located in Moshi Town where you will have an orientation on your trek. The rest of the day can be spent either at the Hotel or exploring Moshi town. You have all the detailes of the trek and I will look at your trekking gears and if something is amiss, I will assist you with how to rent those missing gears from the rental shops.
