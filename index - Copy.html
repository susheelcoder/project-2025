Serengeti National Park: Where The Wild Roams Free

The pride of Tanzania’s safari circuit is Serengeti National Park—characterized by sprawling savannahs, captivating sunsets, and stunning wildlife spectacles. It’s best known for the Great Migration, offering an unparalleled African safari experience bursting with unforgettable game drives, wildlife, and majestic vistas.

Why Visit Serengeti National Park?

The wildlife Serengeti is famous for thundering 1.5 million wildebeests’ migration together with zebras and gazelles, making it the largest of its kind in the world. Situated in Africa, it encompasses about 14,750 sq kilometers. The carpet of grassland supports both grazing and hunting of fierce animals like lions, cheetahs and hyenas, making the area so rich with countless cheetah sporting moments from nature documentaries. And every sighting of predators or river crossings further fuels their thrill.

Most Popular Animals found in Serengeti National Park

Serengeti is famous around the globe owing to the wildlife it possesses and the amazing Great Migration which occurs annually. This national park shelters hosts one of the animal kingdoms spectacles, “The Big Five”, consisting of lions, leopards, buffalo, rhinos and elephants, along with vast rivaling species. Great numbers of zebras, changers, and crocs are preyed on by hyenas, leopards, and cheetahs. Also watched giraffes, hippos, warthogs, and jackals accompany elands, impalas, topped and hartebeests. As well as the park is the habitat to more than 500 bird species like ostriches, vultures, eagles, secretary birds, and crowned cranes. The amount of species in The Serengeti enables it to be considered as one of the best places to have wildlife adventure.

FAQs – Serengeti National Park Safari

When is the best time to visit the Serengeti?

The Serengeti is a year-round destination. The migration usually peaks from June to September (river crossings) and January to March (calving season).

How do I get to Serengeti National Park?

Many visitors fly into Tanzania's main airports and then take a flight or drive to the park. Details depend on your starting point and travel preferences.

What types of accommodation are available?

From luxury lodges and tented camps to budget-friendly options, there's a range of choices to suit every traveler.

Is Serengeti suitable for families?

Yes! Many lodges cater to families with children, offering safari activities and safety measures suitable for all ages.
