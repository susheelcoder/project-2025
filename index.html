Daily activities

Designed to help children realize their potential

Morning Huddles

Children start their day with mindfulness exercises, group discussions, and planning their learning activities. This helps build focus, confidence, and a positive start to the day.

The Basics

Focus on practical life skills, personal care, and foundational Montessori exercises. Children develop independence, concentration, and essential daily skills.

Play Stations

Hands-on play with sensory, creative, and interactive activities. Children explore colors, shapes, textures, and social skills in a safe and engaging environment.

Art & Creativity

Children explore painting, drawing, music, and dance to express their creativity, improve fine motor skills, and develop confidence in self-expression.

Outdoor Learning

Structured outdoor activities including games, gardening, and nature exploration. Children build physical coordination, teamwork, and awareness of the environment.

Mindfulness & Social Skills

Children learn emotional awareness, empathy, and communication skills through group activities, storytelling, and guided reflection exercises.
